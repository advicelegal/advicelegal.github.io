Top 10 Reasons to Seek Help from Utah Legal Services

Home / Blog

Legal issues can be overwhelming, especially when you’re navigating them alone. Whether you’re facing eviction, domestic violence, or unfair employment practices, Utah Legal Services (ULS) is a lifeline for those who can’t afford private attorneys. Here are the top 10 reasons why seeking help from ULS could be the best decision you make.

1. Facing Eviction or Housing Discrimination

The Housing Crisis in Utah

With rising rent prices and a competitive housing market, many Utah residents are at risk of eviction. ULS provides free legal assistance to low-income tenants facing wrongful evictions, unsafe living conditions, or housing discrimination based on race, gender, or disability.

How ULS Can Help

  • Defending against illegal evictions
  • Negotiating with landlords for repairs
  • Fighting housing discrimination under the Fair Housing Act

2. Domestic Violence and Protective Orders

The Silent Epidemic

Domestic violence remains a critical issue, with many victims unable to escape abusive situations due to financial dependence. ULS helps survivors obtain protective orders, secure custody, and navigate divorce proceedings safely.

Legal Support for Survivors

  • Filing restraining orders
  • Assisting with child custody battles
  • Connecting survivors with shelters and resources

3. Unemployment and Wage Theft

The Post-Pandemic Labor Struggle

Many workers face unpaid wages, wrongful termination, or unsafe working conditions. ULS advocates for employees who’ve been exploited, ensuring they receive the wages and benefits they deserve.

Fighting for Workers’ Rights

  • Recovering stolen wages
  • Challenging wrongful termination
  • Addressing workplace discrimination

4. Immigration and Refugee Assistance

Navigating a Complex System

Immigrants and refugees often struggle with legal barriers, from visa applications to deportation threats. ULS offers guidance on asylum claims, DACA renewals, and family reunification.

How ULS Supports Immigrants

  • Assisting with citizenship applications
  • Defending against deportation
  • Providing language-accessible legal aid

5. Healthcare Access and Medical Debt

The Burden of Medical Bills

Medical debt is a leading cause of bankruptcy in the U.S. ULS helps clients dispute unfair medical bills, apply for Medicaid, and fight insurance denials.

Legal Solutions for Healthcare

  • Negotiating medical debt settlements
  • Appealing denied insurance claims
  • Ensuring access to disability benefits

6. Consumer Protection Against Fraud

Scams Targeting Vulnerable Populations

Predatory lenders, identity theft, and fraudulent contractors often target low-income individuals. ULS helps victims recover losses and hold scammers accountable.

Fighting Back Against Fraud

  • Canceling predatory loans
  • Recovering stolen assets
  • Reporting fraudulent businesses

7. Veterans’ Benefits and Legal Aid

Veterans Left Behind

Many veterans struggle to access VA benefits or face wrongful denials. ULS assists with appeals, disability claims, and discharge upgrades.

Serving Those Who Served

  • Securing VA healthcare and pensions
  • Overturning unjust military discharges
  • Connecting vets with housing programs

8. Family Law and Child Custody Battles

Protecting Families in Crisis

Divorce and custody disputes can be emotionally and financially draining. ULS provides low-cost legal support to ensure fair outcomes for parents and children.

Legal Help for Families

  • Establishing paternity and child support
  • Modifying custody agreements
  • Handling guardianship cases

9. Elder Abuse and Senior Rights

The Hidden Abuse of Seniors

Financial exploitation, neglect, and abuse of the elderly are growing concerns. ULS intervenes to protect seniors’ rights and recover stolen assets.

Advocating for the Elderly

  • Stopping financial exploitation
  • Securing proper nursing home care
  • Assisting with estate planning

10. Disability Rights and Accessibility

Fighting for Equal Access

People with disabilities often face discrimination in employment, housing, and public services. ULS enforces ADA compliance and fights for accommodations.

Ensuring Fair Treatment

  • Challenging workplace discrimination
  • Advocating for accessible housing
  • Securing disability benefits

If you’re struggling with any of these issues, Utah Legal Services can provide the support you need—free or at a low cost. Don’t face the legal system alone; reach out and get the help you deserve.
